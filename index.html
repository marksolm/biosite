Hi, I'm Younan Yaquob

About me

I am called Younan ( Jonah ) because I was born in Jonah`s celebration according to the Egyptian Coptic calendar. My family contained five members. I was the second member of my brothers. I grew up in a house which is full of love and sanctification. After some years I have met a nice, lovely girl. When I saw her, I decided to marry her. I got married at age of thirty. After that, I have got my two daughters who bring joy and happiness to our home.


Education

At school, I had met new friends who were clever too. We were in competitions in studies and sports. I have studied hard during high school, and I succeeded to attend a good university. I had studied languages both English and German. I studied hard and got high marks in my studies.


Work

I tried to work in my college as a teacher, but there was no chance to work there for some reasons. I have moved to another city to look for work, and I worked at a famous hotel as a receptionist. My work remained for some months then I worked as a tourist guide. After that, I opened my very first own shop which was offering jewelry to tourists. Then I got my second shop in which I sold papyrus.I had to read much about old ancient Egyptian history to explain any Papyrus (opens in new window) picture. It is interesting to know ancient Egyptian history." Life is interesting when you have a good family, and you achieve yourself.
