Hi, I'M Younan Yaquob

Who I am

I am called younan ( Jonah ), because I was born in Jonah`s celebration according to Egyptian Coptic calendar . My family contained five members. I was the second member among my brothers. I grew up at a house which is full of love and sanctification. After some years I have met a nice, lovely girl. When I saw her, I decided to marry her. I got married at age of thirty. After that, I have got my two daughters who bring joy and happiness to our home.


During my school

At school I had met new friends who were clever too. We were in competitions in studies and sports. I have studied hard during high school and I succeeded to attend a good university. I had studied languages both English and German. I studied hard and got high marks at my studies.


What I do

I tried to work in my college as a teacher, but there was no chance to work there for some reason. I have moved to another city to look for work, and I worked at a famous hotel as a receptionist. My work remained for some months then I worked as a tourist guide. After that, I opened my very first own shop which was offering jewelry to tourists. Then I got my second shop in which I sold papyrus.I had to read much about old ancient Egyptian history to explain any Papyrus picture. It is interesting to know ancient Egyptian history." Life is interesting when you have a good family, and you achieve yourself.
